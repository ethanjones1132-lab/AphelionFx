About Aphelion FXs
Sound is energy. We shape it with intention.

Aphelion FXs is a boutique audio plugin studio building professional-grade effects for music producers, engineers, and sound designers. Every plugin we release is crafted with obsessive attention to both the science and the art of audio processing.

Named after the point in a planet's orbit farthest from the sun, Aphelion represents perspective — the vantage point where you can see the whole picture. We build tools that give you that clarity in your mix.

Based in the mountains of Appalachia. Built for studios everywhere.
